About Our Technology

We are using cutting-edge machine learning models and large-scale data processing systems to continuously improve our predictions and optimize transportation routes.

Machine Learning Models

Our system leverages advanced algorithms such as Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, and Neural Networks. These models help us identify patterns in real-time traffic data and predict optimal bus routes and timings.

Data Sources

We use a combination of GPS data from buses, real-time traffic updates, and weather forecasts. By integrating these data sources, we can ensure that our predictions remain accurate and up-to-date.

Continuous Improvement

Our system continuously learns from past data, using reinforcement learning techniques to improve its accuracy over time. The more data we process, the better our models become.
